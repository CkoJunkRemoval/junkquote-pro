## Driveway Mode

A simplified interface with oversized buttons and minimal typing, optimized for tablets and phones during on-site estimates.

Goals:
- Large touch targets
- One-handed operation
- Fast room-to-room navigation
- Offline support

## Area Pricing

Every job site maintains its own subtotal.

Benefits

- Customer can remove one room and instantly see the new total.
- Crew knows which areas were included.
- Makes upselling easier.
- Keeps estimates organized.

# Customer Success Score

Every company gets an internal health score.

Example:

★★★★★

Based on:

- Estimate completion speed
- Customer response time
- Follow-up rate
- Jobs completed
- Repeat customers

The software coaches owners toward improving their business.
